## Understanding the Timeline

### Timeline Layout
The timeline shows your tasks in chronological order with:
- **Task title** and description
- **Time** and date information
- **Status indicator** (colored ring)
- **Priority level** (if specified)

### Time Grouping
Tasks are automatically grouped by:
- **Today**: Current day tasks
- **Tomorrow**: Next day tasks
- **This Week**: Upcoming week
- **Later**: Future tasks

### Visual Indicators
- **Gray ring**: Upcoming tasks
- **Green ring with checkmark**: Recently completed
- **Black ring**: Archived/completed tasks

## Task Status System

### Three Status Types
1. **Upcoming** (Gray)
   - Tasks that haven't been started
   - Default status for new tasks

2. **Recently Completed** (Green)
   - Tasks completed within the last 24 hours
   - Automatically transitions to "Completed" after 24 hours

3. **Completed** (Black)
   - Archived completed tasks
   - Older than 24 hours or manually archived

### Status Transitions
Tasks automatically transition through statuses:
- **Upcoming** ‚Üí **Recently Completed** (when marked complete)
- **Recently Completed** ‚Üí **Completed** (after 24 hours)

You can also manually change status:
- Mark upcoming tasks as complete
- Reopen completed tasks
- Archive tasks immediately

## Tips and Best Practices

### Writing Effective Task Descriptions
**Be Specific:**
- Include who, what, when, where
- Mention deadlines and priorities
- Add context for better understanding

**Use Natural Language:**
- Write as you would speak
- Don't worry about perfect grammar
- Include relevant details

**Examples of Good Descriptions:**
- "Schedule 30-minute one-on-one with Sarah next Tuesday afternoon to discuss project progress"
- "Buy ingredients for dinner party Saturday: wine, cheese, crackers, and flowers"
- "Prepare presentation slides for client meeting Thursday morning, focusing on Q4 results"

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Usage
**Q: How does Vitordo understand my input?**
A: Vitordo uses advanced AI language models to parse your natural language input and extract task information like titles, times, priorities, and context.

**Q: Can I edit tasks after creating them?**
A: Yes, click the actions menu (‚ãÆ) on any task and select "Edit" to modify details.

**Q: What happens to completed tasks?**
A: Completed tasks remain visible for 24 hours with a green status ring, then automatically archive with a black ring.

### Technical Questions
**Q: Does Vitordo work offline?**
A: Yes, Vitordo caches your tasks locally and queues actions when offline. Everything syncs when you reconnect.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, all data is stored locally in your browser and encrypted. No personal information is sent to external servers without your consent.

**Q: Can I export my tasks?**
A: Yes, you can export tasks in various formats through the settings menu.

### Troubleshooting
**Q: Why aren't my tasks being created?**
A: Check your internet connection and try rephrasing your input more clearly. Make sure to include specific timing information.

**Q: The app seems slow. What can I do?**
A: Try clearing your browser cache, reducing animations in settings, or archiving old completed tasks.

**Q: I lost my tasks. Can I recover them?**
A: Tasks are automatically backed up locally. Check the recovery options in settings or contact support.
